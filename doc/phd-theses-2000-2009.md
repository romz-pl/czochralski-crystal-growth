## Melt Growth — General / Solution Growth (Finite Element / Multiscale Modeling)

1. **Vartak, Abhijeet B.** *Three-Dimensional Modeling of Solution Crystal Growth via the Finite Element Method.* Ph.D. thesis, University of Minnesota, 2001.
   - Advisor group: Jeffrey J. Derby. Developed 3D finite-element models of solution crystal growth (e.g., potassium titanyl phosphate, KTP), including convection-dominated mass transfer algorithms.

2. **Sonda, Paul.** *Development of Model-Based Control for Bridgman Crystal Growth.* Ph.D. thesis, University of Minnesota, ca. 2005.
   - Advisor group: Jeffrey J. Derby / Prodromos Daoutidis. Studied time-dependent flow dynamics and feedback control (crucible rotation, proportional/PI/input–output linearizing controllers) of the vertical Bridgman process.

## Bridgman / Vertical Gradient Freeze (VGF) Growth

3. **Ziegenbalg, Stefan.** *Kontrolle freier Ränder bei der Erstarrung von Kristallschmelzen* [Control of Free Boundaries in the Solidification of Crystal Melts]. Ph.D. thesis, Technische Universität Dresden, 2007.
   - Formulates the VGF solidification process as a two-phase Stefan problem with convection and Lorentz-force-driven flow; develops an adjoint-based optimal control approach for the free (melt/crystal) boundary shape, with numerical experiments on aluminum and GaAs melts.

4. **Lantzsch, Ronny.** *VGF-Kristallzüchtung unter dem Einfluss externer Magnetfelder* [VGF Crystal Growth under the Influence of External Magnetic Fields]. Ph.D. thesis, Technische Universität Bergakademie Freiberg, 2009.
   - Numerical modeling and simulation of magnetic, flow, temperature, and stress fields for GaAs and Ge single-crystal growth by the Vertical Gradient Freeze method under combined static and traveling magnetic fields.

5. **Wunderwald, Ulrike.** *Beeinflussung des Wärme- und Stofftransports bei der Vertical Gradient Freeze-Kristallzüchtung durch ein rotierendes Magnetfeld* [Influence of Heat and Mass Transport on Vertical Gradient Freeze Crystal Growth by a Rotating Magnetic Field]. Ph.D. thesis, Technische Universität Bergakademie Freiberg, 2009.
   - Numerical and experimental study of a rotating magnetic field's effect on heat/mass transport and interface shape during VGF growth of GaAs and Ge crystals.

## Solution Growth from Metallic Melts

6. **Boschert, Stefan Hermann.** *Numerische Simulation zeitabhängiger Wachstumsprozesse bei der Kristallzüchtung aus metallischen Lösungen* [Numerical Simulation of Time-Dependent Growth Processes in Crystal Growth from Metallic Solutions]. Ph.D. thesis, Albert-Ludwigs-Universität Freiburg, 2000.
   - Finite-element/adaptive-mesh simulation of transient crystal growth from metallic solution melts, including moving-boundary (Stefan-type) formulations.

## Phase-Field Modeling of Solidification

7. **Bi, Zhaohui.** *Directional Solidification of a Binary Alloy Using the Phase Field Model.* Ph.D. thesis, Carnegie Mellon University, 2002.
   - Department of Physics. Applies the phase-field method to directional solidification of a binary alloy, contributing to the quantitative phase-field framework for alloy solidification and dendritic growth.

## Wide-Bandgap / Sublimation Growth (SiC, AlN)

8. **Lu, Peng.** *Sublimation Growth of AlN Bulk Crystals and High-Speed CVD Growth of SiC Epilayers, and Their Characterization.* Ph.D. thesis, Kansas State University, 2006.
   - Combines growth-process work with modeling/characterization of AlN bulk crystals produced by physical vapor transport (sublimation-recondensation) and SiC epitaxial layers.
