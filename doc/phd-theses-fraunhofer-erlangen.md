## Confirmed Theses

1. **Fainberg, J.** (2000). *Entwicklung des Computercodes STHAMAS zur globalen Simulation der Kristallzüchtung nach dem Czochralski-Verfahren* [Development of the computer code STHAMAS for the global simulation of Czochralski crystal growth]. PhD thesis, Technical Faculty, University of Erlangen-Nürnberg.

2. **Kurz, M.** (1998, defended 13 July). Doctoral thesis on the development of **CrysVUN++**, a finite-volume software system for numerical modeling and control of industrial crystal growth processes (Vertical Gradient Freeze / Bridgman-type growth). Technical Faculty, University of Erlangen-Nürnberg. *(Published as: Kurz, M. (1998) "Development of CrysVUN++, a software system for numerical modelling and control of industrial crystal growth processes.")*

3. **Metzger, M.** (2000). *Optimal control of industrial crystal growth processes*. PhD thesis, Technical Faculty, University of Erlangen-Nürnberg. (Work on optimal heater temperature–time profile control for VGF-GaAs growth and annealing furnace optimization, in collaboration with the CGL/IISB.)

4. **Hainke, M.** (2005). *Berechnung von Konvektion und Erstarrung metallischer Legierungen mit dem Softwarepaket CrysVUn* [Calculation of convection and solidification of metallic alloys with the software package CrysVUn]. PhD thesis, Fraunhofer IISB / University of Erlangen-Nürnberg. Awarded the Staedtler Foundation Doctoral Prize, 13 October 2005. (Developed a model for the influence of convection on microstructure formation during solidification of technical aluminum alloys, within the ESA/DLR-funded MICAST project.)

5. **Friedrich, J.** (early 1990s). PhD thesis (University of Erlangen-Nürnberg / Crystal Growth Laboratory) investigating gravity-level dependence of segregation-induced non-uniformity in melt-grown crystals, including clarification of the so-called **"magic-g-level"** phenomenon in centrifuge crystal growth experiments.
